Who Are We?

BigMacDaddy Designs is an innovative company located in the "high country of North Carolina. It was founded in 2019 based on a desire to service small businesses specifically in our area. These businesses typically are run by one owner and have less than 5 employees. Even though they do great work often they haven't attended to their online presence with a website. Therefore BigMacDaddy Designs is a competitive and viable option so the small business can attain the internet recognition that it deserves.

Our company has a basic philosophy that involves taking care of the customer. This starts with understanding their needs and then fine tuning our website products to give them the results they desire. It remains our goal to not only meet but to exceed our customers expectations on a daily basis.
